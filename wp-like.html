About The Doctor

Dr. Vladimir Soyfer, DMD

Dr. Vladimir Soyfer places the patient at the heart of every decision, ensuring personalized treatment plans that prioritize comfort, functionality and aesthetics. His goal is not just to treat dental problems, but to improve the overall quality of life for his patients.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major PPO insurance plans including Delta Dental, Aetna, Cigna, and many others. We also offer financing options through CareCredit.

Are you taking new patients?

Yes! We are currently welcoming new patients of all ages to our Lorton, VA office. We reserve emergency slots daily.

What if I'm afraid of the dentist?

Dental anxiety is very common. We specialize in gentle care and offer various comfort options, including sedation dentistry.
