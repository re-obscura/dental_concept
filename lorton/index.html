Meet the Doctor

Dr. Vladimir Soyfer DMD

Your Best Dentist in Lorton

Dr. Vladimir Soyfer places the patient at the heart of every decision, ensuring personalized treatment plans that prioritize comfort, functionality and aesthetics. His goal is not just to treat dental problems, but to improve the overall quality of life for his patients.

Vladimir Soyfer has transformed the way common and complex dental issues are addressed by embracing the latest advances in dental technology. His practice reflects a commitment to innovation from digital imaging and 3D printing to state-of-the-art implant systems. Dr. Soyfer believes that oral health is integral to general health.

Vladimir Soyfer specializes in complex restorations, esthetics, and dental implants. Dr. Vladimir Soyfer holds a Bachelor of Science degree from Ohio University and a Dentistry degree from the University of Pennsylvania. His clinical career began at the Georgetown University Department of Oral Surgery, which provided a solid foundation for his professional growth and experience. Vladimir Soyfer has published over 50 scientific papers on various aspects of oral surgery.
