Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept my insurance?
We accept most major PPO insurance plans including Delta Dental, Aetna, Cigna, and many others. We also offer financing options through CareCredit for patients without insurance or for cosmetic procedures.

Are you taking new patients?
Yes! We are currently welcoming new patients of all ages. We reserve emergency slots daily for urgent needs as well.

What if I'm afraid of the dentist?
Dental anxiety is very common. We specialize in gentle care and offer various comfort options, including sedation dentistry, to ensure your visit is stress-free and pain-free.
