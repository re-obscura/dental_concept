Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept my insurance?

Yes! We accept most PPO insurance plans including Delta, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and many others. Our team will handle all the paperwork for you to maximize your benefits.

I don't have insurance. Can I still come?

Absolutely. We offer an in-house Membership Plan for uninsured patients that covers your cleanings, exams, and X-rays for a low annual fee, plus discounts on treatments.

Do you see children?

We love kids! We recommend bringing your child in for their first visit around age 1 or when their first tooth appears.
