What To Expect At Your First Visit

Our office is a judgment-free zone. Here's what to expect:

  • Reviewing Records: We check past history to provide informed treatment.
  • X-rays: Digital x-rays to look below the surface.
  • Teeth Cleaning: Removal of plaque and tartar buildup.
  • Oral Exam: Dr. Doshi will check the health of your teeth and gums.
  • Treatment Planning: Discuss findings and next steps.
